Our Story

Where We Began

Kauai is a South African health food restaurant chain founded in 1995 in Cape Town. Beginning with a focus on freshly prepared wraps, salads, smoothies, and juices, Kauai quickly gained a loyal following among health-conscious consumers and fitness communities.

Over the past three decades, we have expanded to over 170 locations nationwide, becoming a staple in active spaces like Virgin Active health clubs and major shopping centres.

Our Mission

To nourish South Africans with real, wholesome food that fuels an active and balanced lifestyle, made fresh to order every day.

Our Vision

To be the most loved healthy fast-food brand in South Africa, recognised for quality ingredients, bold flavours, and a commitment to wellness.

Our Core Values

  • Freshness

    Everything is made to order with crisp, high-quality ingredients.

  • Accessibility

    Healthy living shouldn't be complicated or unaffordable.

  • Balance

    Fueling an active lifestyle with macronutrient-aware choices.

  • Sustainability

    Striving toward environmentally friendly practices in all our stores.
